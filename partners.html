Centra Partners

Accredited Management Organization (AMO®)

Centra Partners is an AMO® firm specializing in asset and property management, financial, brokerage, and receivership services for commercial and multifamily properties in Texas and the Southwest for institutional clients and other substantial private owners of real property, U.S. and foreign.

The team includes more than 300 management, leasing, accounting, engineering, and administrative professionals—among them Certified Property Managers (IREM), Certified Public Accountants, Certified Apartment Property Supervisors, and HUD Certified Supervisors. Centra has been recognized by Multifamily Leadership as one of the Best Places to Work Multifamily®.

Track record highlights include leasing over 10 million square feet of commercial space, managing 15 million square feet of commercial space, and oversight of more than 30,000 units of multifamily housing. The firm is active in both national and local Apartment Associations, in addition to BOMA and IREM.

Triad Real Estate Consulting Group

Full-service consulting • Investment analysis • Construction management

Triad Real Estate Consulting Group is a full-service firm with expertise in investment analysis, financial underwriting, asset management, highest-and-best-use analysis, and construction management. The team’s experience spans acquisitions, dispositions, restructuring, and ground-up developments across multifamily, industrial, office, and retail sectors throughout Texas.

With 25+ years in business, Triad manages over 200,000 square feet of office space and provides services including leasing, accounting, engineering, and administrative functions. The group actively consults on ground-up developments and fully underwrites 150+ multifamily properties annually, totaling a value of nearly $3B.

Three-step process:

  • First: Understand objectives and define steps toward achieving them.
  • Second: Execute a disciplined strategy with detailed due diligence.
  • Third: Uphold respect and professionalism to ensure successful transactions.

Triad is committed to building enduring relationships through integrity and consistent execution.
