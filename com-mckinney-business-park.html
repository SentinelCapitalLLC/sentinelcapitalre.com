Investment Overview

McKinney National Business Park is a 71.88-acre Class A, master-planned industrial development with two 75,000 SF buildings delivered and ready for occupancy. The adjacent remaining land, 62.21 acres, can accommodate up to nine additional buildings ranging from ±50,000 to ±300,000 SF.

Located within the #1 Best Place to Live (Money Magazine), the park provides close proximity to thriving retail and housing. Regional access is enhanced by the new Harry McKillop Parkway, a four-lane divided road designed for industrial traffic.

The park’s professional team supports an efficient process across planning, architecture, and construction to provide swift occupancy.
