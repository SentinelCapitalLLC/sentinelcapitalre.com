About Us

Sentinel Capital was formed in 2012 as a real estate investment sponsor specializing in the acquisition and asset management of mid-market multifamily assets throughout the State of Texas.

Sentinel has followed a highly disciplined investment strategy sponsoring deals throughout the State, with a current AUM of over $350MM across 1,600+ multifamily units and 750K SF of commercial acquisitions and developments.

Over the past decade, Sentinel has owned over 4,000 multi-family units and 1.2 million of commercial space – with monetized assets realized of over $325MM. Sentinel’s executive team has over 40-years of Texas real estate experience, with a combined experience of +100-years.

Sentinel’s seasoned professional relationships give access to proprietary deal flow. Combined with our specialized and hands-on focus of operations, this has created trusted investor relationships and outsized returns for our clients. Sentinel utilizes a top-down market approach, identifying submarkets that benefit from long-term demand drivers, followed by a value-creation assessment of specific investment opportunities therein.

Vision
Our vision is to grow from a trusted family office into a premier, mid-sized real estate investment firm recognized for its disciplined strategy, long–term value creation, and enduring partnerships. We aim to be a partner of choice for all existing and future clients, delivering consistent performance and most importantly, consistent transparency. Rooted in entrepreneurial drive, we are committed to upholding the reputation that we have all so carefully constructed.
Mission
Our mission is to identify, acquire, and manage high-quality commercial real estate assets that generate lasting value for our investors and the communities we serve. We operate with integrity, precision, and purpose—leveraging our deep market insight, disciplined investment approach, and entrepreneurial mindset to deliver superior risk-adjusted returns. Guided by a commitment to excellence and an unwavering dedication to reputation, we build enduring partnerships and create resilient portfolios designed for long-term growth.
Values
Integrity: Every decision we make is rooted in honesty, accountability, and transparency. Our reputation is built on trust — earned through consistency and ethical conduct.

Uniqueness: We embrace innovative thinking and unconventional strategies that distinguish us in a competitive market. Our ability to identify underappreciated assets and create tailored investment solutions reflects our entrepreneurial DNA.

Zeal: We are passionate about real estate, driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence. Our enthusiasm fuels bold action, meticulous execution, and an unwavering commitment to achieving exceptional outcomes.

Resiliency: In a constantly evolving market, we remain steadfast—navigating challenges with discipline and agility. Our long-term perspective empowers us to build enduring value across market cycles.
